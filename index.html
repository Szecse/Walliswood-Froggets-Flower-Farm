DAHLIA SPECIALIST & GROWER

The Art of Nurturing Extraordinary Dahlias

Froggetts Flower Farm is born from a deep fascination with the endless shapes, vibrant colours, and magnificent presence of British-grown dahlias.

Led by Clare’s passion and expertise, our West Sussex farm has become a dedicated sanctuary for these spectacular summer blooms. From carefully conditioning rare varieties to cultivating the soil with sustainable, peat-free methods, we pour heart and science into every single tuber we grow.

We believe that dahlias are more than just flowers—they are a celebration of British nature at its most dramatic. Whether you are a flower lover looking for the perfect fresh bunch, an aspiring grower attending our summer masterclasses, or a local volunteer, we are here to share the joy and secrets of dahlia cultivation with you.

WORKSHOPS & EDUCATION

Learning & Growing Together

Froggetts Flower Farm is rooted in community and sharing the joy of nature. Join us on the farm to learn seasonal floristry crafts or take part in our dedicated educational and volunteering programmes.

  • Dahlia & Creative Workshops: From summer Dahlia Masterclasses and bouquet conditioning to festive winter wreath making.
  • School Visits: Engaging, nature-focused educational days exploring biodiversity and British flowers.
  • DofE Volunteering: Empowering local youth through official Duke of Edinburgh Award volunteering opportunities.
