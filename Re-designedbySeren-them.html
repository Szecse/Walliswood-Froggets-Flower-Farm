The Art of Nurturing Extraordinary Dahlias.

Froggetts Flower Farm is a dedicated Surrey sanctuary for British-grown dahlias. From carefully conditioning rare varieties to cultivating the earth with sustainable, peat-free methods, we pour both heart and science into every single tuber we grow.

Our organic dahlias bloom beautifully from mid-July through late October. If you are looking for the absolute peak of color and variety, we highly recommend booking your visit or Pick Your Own tea afternoon between August and September.

Yes, we provide sustainable, fresh-cut flower deliveries to our local community surrounding Walliswood, Loxwood, and the wider Surrey area. You can also join our weekly Community Flower Club for regular field-to-vase arrangements.

Absolutely. We are deeply committed to organic horticulture and biodiversity. Every single tuber and stem is cultivated using 100% peat-free compost, and we completely avoid synthetic chemicals to maintain a healthy ecosystem for local wildlife.
